How the draft works

Construction doctrine

1. Citizen Credits become district influence.

Every wallet with lifetime claimed Citizen Credits is mapped into a district using a deterministic wallet signature. The same wallet always strengthens the same district.

2. Relative strength unlocks new structures.

District growth is measured against the current leading district. Falling behind does not erase progress, but it does slow landmark unlocks until the district catches up.

3. City eras are driven by ecosystem-wide credits.

As total observed Citizen Credits rise, Alpha City graduates from excavation to skyline status. This gives the whole community a shared macro objective while districts battle locally.
